  <h3 style="margin-top: 0; color: var(--green);">Why Kids Protocols Matter</h3>
  
  <p><strong>For ADHD/Autism Parents:</strong></p>
  <ul>
    <li>Reduces cognitive load of remembering routines</li>
    <li>Provides structure when executive function fails</li>
    <li>Prevents decision fatigue during high-stress moments</li>
    <li>Creates predictability for neurodivergent kids</li>
    <li>Manages guilt by having "good enough" standards documented</li>
  </ul>

  <ul>
    <li>Reduces conflict through clear expectations</li>
    <li>Provides consistency across two households</li>
    <li>Documents routines for smooth handoffs</li>
    <li>Creates communication boundaries</li>
    <li>Tracks what works and what doesn't</li>
  </ul>
  
  <p><strong>For Kids:</strong></p>
  <ul>
    <li>Predictable routines reduce anxiety</li>
    <li>Clear expectations prevent confusion</li>
    <li>Smooth transitions between houses</li>
    <li>Consistent structure = more fun time</li>
    <li>Less time spent on logistics = more quality time</li>
  </ul>
