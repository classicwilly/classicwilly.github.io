# Command Center

The Command Center serves as the hub for managing your neurodivergent life. Here, you can access tools, protocols, and resources designed to streamline your daily routines and enhance productivity.

## Features

1. **Task Management**: Organize tasks with clear priorities based on the established hierarchy.
2. **Protocol Access**: Quick links to Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for daily routines.
3. **Resource Library**: Access to tools and resources that support your unique needs.

## Getting Started

- Familiarize yourself with the **Dual-Mode Life Structure** to maximize efficiency.
- Review the **Priority Hierarchy** to ensure essential needs are met first.

## Next Steps

- Explore the **Workspace** page for detailed task management strategies.
- Refer to the **Fix-It Blueprint** for troubleshooting common challenges.

This Command Center is designed to adapt to your needs, providing a structured yet flexible approach to life management.
