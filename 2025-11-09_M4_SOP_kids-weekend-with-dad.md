## üò∞ WHEN YOU FEEL SAD OR WORRIED

### It's Okay to Feel Big Feelings
Sometimes you might feel:
- Sad about Mom and Dad
- Worried about going back and forth
- Mad about things changing
- Scared of something
- Just... not okay

**This is NORMAL. You are NOT in trouble for having feelings.**

### What to Do When You Feel Bad
1. **Find Dad** - Tell him "I need help" or "I feel sad"
2. **Hug Time** - Ask for a hug (as long as you want)
3. **Talk About It** - Dad will listen (no judgment!)
4. **Quiet Space** - Go to your room for alone time if you need it
5. **Comfort Item** - Grab your stuffed animal, blanket, or favorite toy

### Special Tools for Big Feelings
- **Breathing Ball** - On your shelf, squeeze it when worried
- **Feelings Chart** - Point to how you feel (on your wall)
- **Calm Down Corner** - Beanbag chair with soft things
- **Call Mom** - If you miss Mom, Dad will help you call

### Magic Words
When you're upset, try saying:
- "I need a break"
- "I need help"
- "I feel [sad/mad/scared]"
- "Can we talk?"

**Dad will ALWAYS stop and listen.**
