<h2 id="feeling-sad">üò∞ WHEN YOU FEEL SAD OR WORRIED</h2>

### It's Okay to Feel Big Feelings
Sometimes you might feel:
- Sad about Mom and Dad
- Worried about going back and forth
- Mad about things changing
- Scared of something
- Just... not okay

**This is NORMAL. You are NOT in trouble for having feelings.**

1. **Find Dad** - Tell him "I need help" or "I feel sad"
